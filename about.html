About Us

Our dojo supports disciplined training, thoughtful progression,
and a strong culture rooted in Shito-Ryu karate tradition.

At KEN-EI-MABUNI SHITO-RYU KARATE, we build martial artists who are calm under pressure, disciplined in practice, and confident in every aspect of life.

Led by Renshi Yogesh More and supported by experienced teachers.

What We Do

We teach structured classes that guide students from their
first stance to advanced Shito-Ryu practice,
combining traditional technique with
modern training principles,
Our goal is to build confident and disciplined martial artists.

What We Provide

Our dojo provides training in
self-defense,
fitness and flexibility,
grading progress and
a welcoming space for students at every level.

History & Legacy

Shito-Ryu karate is carried forward through disciplined instructors and passionate students. Our program preserves the legacy while encouraging contemporary growth.

Roots in Tradition

Shito-Ryu is known for its wide range of kata, kumite, practical techniques, and strong emphasis on both hard and soft movements.

Modern Dojo Culture

We honor the heritage of karate while teaching training methods that support today's students and families.
