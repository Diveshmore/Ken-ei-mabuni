APPLY FOR AFFILIATION

Our Affiliation Network

These organizations play an important role in our dojo's growth, karate standards, and recognition across the region and beyond.

KEN-EI-MABUNI SHITO-RYU KARATE-DO ASSOCIATION (KMSKA)

Our primary affiliation dedicated to preserving and promoting authentic Shito-Ryu karate-do across Maharashtra.

KARATE-DO ASSOCIATION OF MAHARASHTRA (KAM)

KAM regulates karate activities across Maharashtra with a strong commitment to discipline, excellence, and ethical training.

KARATE INDIA ORGANIZATION (KIO)

The national karate body recognized by the Government of India, promoting karate education and safe training nationwide.

WORLD KARATE FEDERATION (WKF)

WKF is the international governing body for karate recognized by the International Olympic Committee and responsible for global karate development.

ASIAN KARATE FEDERATION (AKF)

AKF oversees karate activities across Asia and strengthens unity and excellence for member countries.

SOUTH ASIAN KARATE FEDERATION (SAKF)

SAKF promotes karate development and competition across South Asia, fostering unity and excellence among member nations.

KARATE DO SHITOKAI MURAYAMA

Karate-Do Shitokai Murayama promotes traditional Shito-Ryu karate, discipline, self-defense, fitness, and international-level martial arts training.

ASIA PACIFIC SHITORYU KARATE-DO FEDERATION

The Asia Pacific Shitoryu Karate-Do Federation promotes the development and practice of traditional Shito-Ryu karate across the Asia-Pacific region.

WORLD SHITO-RYU KARATE FEDERATION

The World Shito-Ryu Karate Federation promotes the development and practice of traditional Shito-Ryu karate globally.

ALL INDIA SHITORYU KARATE-DO UNION

The All India Shitoryu Karate-Do Union promotes the development and practice of traditional Shito-Ryu karate across India.
